Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sleeping Goddesses Saga automated content publishing system ("the App") handles information when it interacts with third-party platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Threads) on behalf of its sole operator, Alexey Tarapovsky, author of the Sleeping Goddesses tetralogy.

1. Scope

The App is a private, single-author tool used solely to publish promotional video and text content for the Sleeping Goddesses book series to the author's own social media accounts. It is not intended for, nor offered to, any third-party end users.

2. Information We Access

  • OAuth access tokens issued by TikTok, Meta, and other platforms to the App's authorized creator account.
  • Public profile information (username, account ID) of the author's authorized account, required by the platform APIs to verify posting permissions.
  • Media (video, image, text) files generated by the author for publication.

3. How We Use Information

Tokens are stored only on the author's private server and used exclusively to call TikTok and other platform APIs to publish content authored by Alexey Tarapovsky. No data is shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising.

4. Data Retention

OAuth refresh tokens are rotated on every exchange and stored locally on the author's server. The author may revoke access at any time from his TikTok account settings, after which all access ceases.
